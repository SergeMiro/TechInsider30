Data recovery

1

We are utilizing an API Telegram to extract posts from the Telegram channels we've chosen. These posts are the ones that have garnered the highest view count within the last 24 hours. Through a series of carefully crafted requests, we retrieve valuable insights into user engagement and content popularity. By harnessing this data, we gain a comprehensive understanding of the most impactful posts circulating across our selected channels. This process allows us to stay informed about emerging trends and relevant discussions, aiding us in making informed decisions for our engagement strategies.

Data translation

2

We are leveraging the power of an API to extract posts from the Telegram channels we have chosen. These posts represent the ones that have amassed the highest view count over the course of the last 24 hours. Through a meticulously crafted sequence of requests, we retrieve invaluable insights into user engagement and the popularity of the content. With these insights in hand, we embark on a journey of translation, utilizing the Google Translate API to render these posts into English. This multistep process ensures that we not only remain informed about the latest trends and discussions but also make this information accessible to a wider English-speaking audience. Our efforts are dedicated to bridging the language gap and expanding the reach of these impactful posts across linguistic boundaries.
